Class 12 - Physics Important Questions

Q1. State Coulomb's Law.
The force between two point charges is directly proportional to product of charges and inversely proportional to square of distance between them. F = k(q1*q2)/r²
Q2. What is electric field intensity?
Force experienced by a unit positive charge placed at that point. E = F/q. Unit: N/C or V/m

Class 12 - Chemistry Important Questions

Q1. Define Mole Concept.
One mole is amount of substance that contains 6.022 × 10²³ particles. Avogadro Number = 6.022 × 10²³
Q2. What is Molarity?
Number of moles of solute present in 1 litre of solution. M = moles of solute / Volume of solution in L
